Dobrodošli u Restoran

A Culinary Journey Through Traditional Sarajevo

Restoran is more than just a restaurant; it's a sanctuary of Bosnian heritage located on the main pedestrian street of Sarajevo. We specialize in authentic traditional food, where every dish tells a story of our cultural roots and family recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're a local seeking the comfort of home or a traveler looking for the best place to eat in Sarajevo, our non-smoking, baby-friendly environment offers a premium yet authentic experience that captures the true spirit of Bosnia.
